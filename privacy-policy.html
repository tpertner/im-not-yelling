I'm Not Yelling, You're Yelling™
Privacy Policy & Intellectual Property Notice
ZERO DATA COLLECTION
This app does not collect, store, record, transmit, or share any of your personal data, audio data, or usage data. Ever.

1. Introduction

I'm Not Yelling, You're Yelling™ ("the App") is a conversational awareness tool developed and owned by Tracy Pertner ("Developer"). This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding information when you use the App.

2. Information We Collect

We collect no information whatsoever. This includes: no audio or voice recordings; no personal information; no device identifiers; no location data; no usage or analytics data; no cookies or local storage; no crash reports or diagnostics; no third-party tracking of any kind; no advertising data; no biometric, health, financial, or contact data.

3. Microphone Access

The App requests microphone access solely to analyze ambient volume levels in real time. Audio is processed entirely on your device using the device's built-in audio APIs. It is converted to numerical volume levels only. No speech, words, or sounds are interpreted, transcribed, or stored.

No audio is ever recorded, saved, cached, or transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. When you stop a session, the microphone stream is immediately and permanently terminated. You may revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings.

Volume only — no audio is ever recorded.

4. Third-Party Services

The App contains zero third-party tracking code. No analytics, no ad networks, no crash reporting, no social media integrations, no cloud storage. Because no data is collected, there is no data to share. We do not sell, rent, or transfer any user data to anyone.

5. Network Activity

The App makes zero network connections. It functions entirely offline with no internet required. There are no background requests, heartbeat signals, update checks, or telemetry.

6. Local Storage

The App stores a small number of non-personal preferences on your device (such as whether you have completed the onboarding walkthrough). These contain no personal information, no audio data, and no usage history. They never leave your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

7. Data Retention

Because no personal data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete. Session statistics exist only in temporary memory and are permanently discarded when the session ends. No session data persists between sessions.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from any user, including children under 13. It is compliant with COPPA, GDPR, and equivalent children's privacy laws worldwide.

9. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, CPRA, and others), your data rights are satisfied by default because we collect no personal data.

10. Security

The App uses a zero-data architecture. Because nothing personal is collected, stored, or transmitted, there is no data that could be compromised.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will notify users through the App or app store listing.
